Reincarnate Prototype - Prototype download

Created in collaboration with two other Qantm students in two weeks for a Iron Dev design challenge.

Reincarnate is a 2D platform game designed around the concept of rebirth. Art and play style is similar to the 2d time manipulation game Braid. The Alpha prototype demonstrates art style, a few simple puzzles and the intended flow of the game.

My role in the prototype development was concepting, level and puzzle design, documentation and team management.

Avalon: The Fallen - Game Download

Final project for Qantm College in 2010.

Avalon: The Fallen is a Celtic themed adventure/survival horror game created using the UDK engine. The game is set in a dark eerie supernatural world based on European mythology where even friendly looking mushrooms will try to lure you to a long painful death.

My role in the project is quality assurance which mainly focuses on testing; though I have had a hand in quite a few aspects of the game including design, props, GUI elements and even a few multimedia promotional items.
