Cannonize

Cannonize is a two player turn-based strategy prototype that focuses on the clever placement of cogs.

A player places cogs into the battlefield. If a cog is placed next to a cannon, the player claims that cannon for their own and it will fire when the round ends. The player can hurt themselves with their own cannons, and the player who outlives the other is the winner.

Carcinoma

Created using DarkBASIC Pro, Carcinoma is a serious, educational game aimed at educating medical students about skin cancer identification.

The player first examines an image of a patients skin, identifies if the patient has a cancer, and what it is. The player then chooses the appropriate treatment and takes control of Captain Chemo, who wields their treatment option inside the patient's veins. The effectiveness of Captain Chemo's weapon is based upon the player's choice of treatment, as they strive to save the patient.
