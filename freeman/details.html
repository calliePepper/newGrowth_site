About Me

Greetings! I am a game designer from little ol' Brisbane town.

I design game projects and especially like to focus on the sound and music, though my talents extend to level design and story writing as well.

I've always enjoyed the notion of the virtual world that comes with video games, especially well designed games that immerse the player for hours at a time. I enjoy making games, and seek to make the most of my experiences in gaming.

I am very much a retro console gamer. I especially love old Nintendo games and am greatly inspierd by them, my favourite games being the Metroid, Mega Man and Zelda series.

That doesn't stop me enjoying modern games though, especially the later works of Dice, Bethesda and Ubisoft.
